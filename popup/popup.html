Fast Clicker

Automate your clicks faster

Shortcut

Ctrl + ⇧ + Y

Instructions

  1. Use the key combination above to launch the tool.
  2. Move your mouse where you want to click (active tab).
  3. Use the key combination again to start auto-clicking.
  4. Use it a third time to stop.
  • A visual indicator appears in the bottom-left corner to help you.
  • After the first activation on a page, a single use is enough to start/stop auto-clicking.
